Welcome to my page! I work on video editing and multimodal learning, with a focus on vision-language alignment and generative models for controllable video transformation.

I am an undergraduate researcher at the Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS), where I explore multimodal generation and video understanding. My recent projects involve attention-injection-based video editing and cross-modal alignment frameworks.

I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Junyu Gao, whose guidance has greatly shaped my research in video generation and alignment methods.
